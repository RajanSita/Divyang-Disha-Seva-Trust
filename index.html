Our Mission

"Divyang Disha Seva Trust is a compassionate organization dedicated to creating a world where individuals with diverse abilities lead fulfilling lives with dignity and inclusion. Our mission is to provide holistic support, empowerment, and advocacy for the differently-abled community, fostering a society that values and respects every unique journey."

  • Key Initiatives:
    1. Accessible Education Programs: Offering inclusive educational opportunities tailored to the needs of differently-abled individuals, ensuring they receive quality learning experiences.
    2. Empowerment Workshops: Conducting workshops and skill-building programs to empower members of the disabled community, promoting self-reliance and confidence.
    3. Healthcare Access Initiatives: Facilitating access to healthcare services, rehabilitation, and assistive devices to enhance the overall well-being of individuals with diverse abilities.
    4. Inclusive Employment Opportunities: Collaborating with businesses to create employment opportunities that cater to the strengths and talents of differently-abled individuals, fostering economic independence.
    5. Community Awareness Campaigns: Engaging in awareness campaigns to challenge stereotypes, eliminate stigma, and promote understanding and acceptance of diversity within society.

Vision: "To be the beacon of change in promoting inclusivity, breaking barriers, and uplifting the lives of differently-abled individuals, creating a society where every person, regardless of ability, contributes and thrives."

Values:

  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and ensuring equal opportunities for all.
  • Empathy: Fostering understanding and compassion in all interactions.
  • Dignity: Upholding the dignity and worth of every individual.
  • Collaboration: Working together with communities, stakeholders, and partners to achieve common goals.

Impact Stories

Rakesh's Journey

In the heart of bustling Delhi, Rakesh Suri, a tenacious young man with an unwavering spirit, embarked on a transformative odyssey through the corridors of knowledge and empowerment provided by our organization. Born with a visual impairment, Rakesh faced challenges that would daunt many. However, armed with determination and an unyielding spirit, he enrolled in our specialized education program. Here, amidst accessible learning materials and the guidance of dedicated educators, Rakesh's academic journey blossomed into a beacon of resilience. His journey extended beyond the classrooms. Rakesh actively participated in skill development workshops, honing his talents and acquiring skills that extended far beyond the limitations society tried to impose. Through coding sessions and assistive technology workshops, Rakesh not only adapted to the digital world but emerged as a pioneer, showcasing that innovation knows no bounds. As the chapters of his education unfolded, a new opportunity awaited Rakesh in the tech industry. Securing a position in a forward-thinking tech company, he not only demonstrated his technical prowess but shattered the glass ceiling that often limits those with disabilities. Rakesh's employment success was not just a personal triumph but a victory for inclusivity, proving that diverse abilities enhance, rather than hinder, the workplace. Rakesh's story, from the classrooms to corporate corridors, became a narrative of inspiration. His success reverberated beyond his individual triumph, challenging preconceived notions and encouraging a paradigm shift towards a more inclusive society. Rakesh Suri became a living testament to the potential that lies within every individual, regardless of their physical abilities. In the heart of Delhi, Rakesh's journey became a source of inspiration, fostering a community that values diversity and embraces the extraordinary capabilities within each person. His story echoes through the streets, challenging stereotypes, and promoting inclusivity, leaving an indelible mark on the city and the minds of those who dared to dream beyond limitations.

Amrita's Progress

In the bustling streets of Delhi, a tiny spark of resilience named Amrita Kumari emerged, weaving a tale of triumph over adversity through the embrace of our organization's early intervention programs. Amrita, a young child with special needs, embarked on a journey that would not only transform her life but also illuminate the paths of possibility for those around her. Our specialized education and therapy programs became the nurturing grounds for Amrita's growth, providing her with the tools to navigate a world that often seemed challenging. Within the walls of our programs, Amrita discovered a canvas to paint her unique story. With access to specialized education tailored to her needs, she unfolded her potential like the petals of a blooming flower. Each lesson became a stepping stone, and every therapy session a brushstroke, contributing to the masterpiece that was Amrita's progress. Remarkable strides were made in Amrita's communication and social skills. What were once barriers became bridges, connecting her with a world that had previously seemed distant. The joy radiating from her smile became a testament to the transformative power of early intervention, not only in the life of the individual but in the hearts of those who witnessed her journey. Amrita's story became a beacon of hope for her family. The tears that were once shed in uncertainty transformed into tears of joy as they witnessed the extraordinary progress their child made. Amrita, once seen as a child with special needs, became a source of inspiration, proving that every child, regardless of their abilities, holds within them a universe of untapped potential. Beyond her immediate circle, Amrita's journey became a narrative that resonated with the broader community. Her progress inspired others to believe in the innate capabilities of every child, irrespective of societal labels. The ripple effect of her story touched the lives of families facing similar challenges, instilling hope and encouraging a collective belief in the limitless possibilities that lie within each child. In the tapestry of Delhi's vibrant culture, Amrita Kumari's journey became a thread, weaving a story of resilience, progress, and the boundless potential inherent in every child, lighting up the city with the glow of possibilities yet to be explored.

Programs/Services

Education

Our educational programs are designed to create inclusive learning environments, ensuring that every child, regardless of their abilities, has access to quality education tailored to their unique needs. We provide specialized educators, adaptive resources, and personalized learning plans to foster academic success and personal growth.

Healthcare

In the realm of healthcare, we are committed to providing comprehensive services to enhance the overall well-being of disabled individuals. Our healthcare programs include regular check-ups, specialized therapies (physical, occupational, and speech), and the provision of assistive devices. We prioritize holistic care that addresses both physical and mental health, promoting a healthy and fulfilling life.

Vocational Training

Empowering disabled individuals for the workforce is a key focus of our vocational training programs. We offer a range of skill development workshops, job training sessions, and internship opportunities. Our goal is to equip individuals with practical skills and knowledge, paving the way for meaningful careers and financial independence.

Counseling and Support

Emotional well-being is integral to overall health. Our counseling and support services provide a safe space for individuals and their families to discuss challenges, explore coping mechanisms, and receive guidance. Our qualified counselors work to enhance mental health and contribute to a supportive community.

Social Inclusion Events

We organize and participate in various social inclusion events, fostering a sense of community and breaking down societal barriers. These events provide opportunities for individuals to connect, share experiences, and celebrate achievements, contributing to a more inclusive and accepting society.

Our diverse range of programs reflects our commitment to addressing the multifaceted needs of disabled individuals. By providing comprehensive educational, healthcare, vocational, counseling, and social inclusion support, we aim to create a more inclusive and equitable society.
